Quivro Design Style Description

Modern playful SaaS design with a clean futuristic education-tech aesthetic.

The interface should feel:

Friendly and approachable like a learning platform.
Energetic and engaging like a game.
Professional and trustworthy like a business SaaS product.
Simple and intuitive with minimal visual noise.
Visual Style
Minimalistic flat design
Rounded corners and soft geometric shapes
Smooth curves instead of sharp edges
Clean spacing with plenty of whitespace
Modern card-based layouts
Simple icons with consistent stroke widths
Clear visual hierarchy
Color Palette

Primary colors:

Vibrant electric blue
Purple gradients
Cyan accents

Supporting colors:

Clean white backgrounds
Soft gray surfaces
Dark navy text

The colors should feel:

Modern
Digital
Optimistic
Interactive

Avoid:

Dark corporate themes
Overly serious colors
Excessive gradients
Too many colors at once
Typography

Use:

Rounded modern sans-serif fonts
Medium to bold weights
Friendly but professional appearance

Typography should feel similar to:

Inter
SF Pro Display
Nunito Sans
Circular
Poppins

Text should be:

Large and readable
High contrast
Comfortable on mobile screens
Components

UI components should have:

Cards

Rounded corners (16–24px radius)
Soft borders
Minimal shadows or no shadows
Large icons
Clear information grouping

Buttons

Pill-shaped or softly rounded
Bold text
Bright primary colors
Small playful animations

Icons

Simple geometric icons
Rounded line style
Consistent thickness
Friendly appearance
Overall Brand Feeling

Think:

Kahoot + Duolingo + Notion + Linear

A platform where learning feels like a game.

The user should feel:

"This is easy, fun, modern, and exciting."

Not:

"This is another boring school system."
